MedMemo

MedMemo is a web based hospital communication platform built to streamline and modernize how physicians and nurses connect. Instead of wasted time tracking down the right person or missed calls with no context, MedMemo delivers clear, real-time communication that lets physicians know exactly who is reaching out and why. With a clean, intuitive interface, seamless message delivery, and strong security at its core, the application helps care teams respond faster, reduce friction, and stay focused on what matters most, patient care.
